# Evidence and Message Inputs

This directory stores source material used to design and test the Equitable Immunisation Monitoring prototype.

## Safety Status

Nothing in `evidence/drafts/` is approved for public distribution, broadcast, or use as official Ministry of Health communication. Draft material may inform demonstration templates only after schedule reconciliation and visible labelling.

## Included Files

### Demonstration Draft

`drafts/uganda-immunisation-vht-radio-scripts-en-lg.md`

- English and draft Luganda VHT talking points and radio scripts.
- Provided by the project owner as source-aligned demonstration text.
- Not Ministry-approved.
- Luganda has not been professionally or institutionally reviewed.
- Contains schedule statements that conflict with the newer 2025 schedule image.

## Review Rules

Before a message template enters the application:

1. Reconcile vaccine, age, dose, and timing claims with the current schedule.
2. Remove official attribution unless formally approved.
3. Label the content as a demonstration draft.
4. Keep a source reference with the template.
5. Require human review before campaign approval.
6. Do not publish Luganda text without qualified language and programme review.

## Known Reconciliation Items

- Replace the outdated “five visits before the first birthday” summary with language that directs caregivers to the current Ministry of Health schedule.
- Replace the two-dose HPV statement with the current one-dose schedule for 10-year-old girls, subject to source verification at time of use.
- Review malaria vaccine contacts and the 18-month contact before producing schedule-based messages.
- Replace “This message is brought to you by the Ministry of Health” with a demonstration disclaimer.
