# Feature Research

## Product Promise

Help an immunisation programme manager move from aggregate evidence to a transparent, review-ready outreach action for a selected Uganda district or subcounty.

## Table Stakes

### Evidence Overview

- Wakiso, Mubende, and Kasese comparison.
- First-dose coverage, third-dose coverage, dropout, and available zero-dose indicators.
- Period and source shown beside every value.
- Clear labels for published, synthetic, entered, and generated information.

### Transparent Prioritisation

- RED/REC category derived from visible thresholds.
- Calculation details available without leaving the page.
- Missing-data and denominator warnings.
- No claim that coverage identifies the cause of under-vaccination.

### District Detail

- Trend or period comparison where published data permits.
- Access and utilisation interpretation.
- Evidence summary from the Uganda rapid assessment.
- Source links and limitations.

### Barrier Capture

- User selects an observed barrier rather than accepting an inferred cause.
- Barrier families include confidence/knowledge, social influence, motivation, service access, and service experience.
- Optional notes remain local to the browser and contain no personal data.

### Outreach Package

- SMS or WhatsApp draft.
- Short radio announcement.
- Health-worker or VHT talking points.
- Seven-day activity outline.
- Editable content with a visible AI/template-generated label.
- Review confirmation before approval.

### Campaign Tracker

- Draft, Under Review, Approved, Active, and Completed states.
- District, barrier, owner placeholder, created date, and status history.
- Local demonstration persistence.

### Evidence Assistant

- Botpress chatbot grounded in selected approved sources.
- Suggested questions focused on interpreting evidence and planning demand actions.
- No-answer and safety response.

## Differentiators

- Separates **where** from **why** instead of asking AI to invent explanations.
- Uses a recognised access/utilisation categorisation.
- Makes provenance visible at indicator and recommendation level.
- Treats human review as a product feature, not a disclaimer.
- Connects monitoring and communication in one short workflow.

## Presentation-Critical Features

The live demo must reliably support:

1. Open overview.
2. Identify a Category 4 or otherwise high-attention area.
3. Inspect the reasons in the calculation.
4. Select an observed demand barrier.
5. Generate an outreach package.
6. Mark it Under Review.
7. Ask the chatbot one grounded question.

## Later Features

- Real DHIS2 import.
- MICS 2026–27 data when published.
- Multilingual reviewed message libraries.
- Google Sheets or approved workflow integration.
- Geographic maps.
- Authenticated collaboration and audit log.
- Campaign outcome monitoring.
