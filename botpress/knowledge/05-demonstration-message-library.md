# English Demonstration Message Library

## Status

Every message in this file is a **DEMONSTRATION DRAFT — NOT APPROVED FOR PUBLICATION OR BROADCAST**.

The drafts are designed to show the application's workflow. They require review by the appropriate Uganda immunisation programme and communication authorities before any real use.

Do not add Ministry of Health attribution to generated messages unless explicit approval has been obtained.

## General Routine Immunisation Draft

Immunisation helps protect children from serious vaccine-preventable diseases. Parents and caregivers should follow the current Uganda Ministry of Health immunisation schedule, keep the child's immunisation card safe, and bring it to each visit. For current information, speak to a health worker or VHT.

## Missed Visit Draft

If a child has missed a vaccination visit, a caregiver should contact the nearest health facility or outreach service. A qualified health worker can review the child's card and provide guidance on the appropriate catch-up schedule.

## Reminder Draft

Reminder: please bring your child's immunisation card to the next vaccination visit at [LOCATION] on [DATE]. For questions, contact [APPROVED LOCAL CONTACT].

## VHT Talking Points Draft

- Ask the caregiver whether the child has an immunisation card.
- Encourage the caregiver to follow the current Ministry of Health schedule.
- Explain that a missed visit should be discussed with a qualified health worker.
- Share the confirmed date and location of the next service.
- Listen to the caregiver's concern without dismissing it.
- Refer schedule, eligibility, safety, or adverse-event questions to a qualified health worker.
- Do not collect or repeat personal information in the demonstration application.
